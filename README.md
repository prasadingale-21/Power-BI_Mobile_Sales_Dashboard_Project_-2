# Power-BI_Mobile_Sales_Dashboard_Project_-2
I have prepared mobile sales dashboard in Power BI


* Designed and deployed an interactive Mobile Sales Dashboard in Power BI, integrating multi-dimensional filters (time period, brand, model, payment method) to enable dynamic, user-driven insights.

* Visualized key performance metrics including Total Sales (₹769M), Quantity Sold (19K), and Average Price (₹40.11K) using custom cards, bar charts, and line graphs for executive-level reporting.

* Implemented geo-mapping and temporal analysis to highlight sales distribution by city and monthly quantity trends, enhancing regional and seasonal strategy planning.

* Delivered actionable insights on customer behavior and transaction patterns through segmentation by payment method, day-of-week sales, and customer ratings, driving data-backed business decisions.
